COMPANY | DESCRIPTION

XØ Projects Inc (XØ:p) conceives, plans, designs and crafts structures and experiences which are mindful, practical, artfull and sustainable in all their aspects, including the methods and processes making them manifest.

SERVICES | ACTIVITIES

XØ:p's professional engagements involve overlapping activities in art, design and commerce, and include: planning, architecture and design; property development and management; production of visual and performing arts projects related to the built environment; and communication strategy and design to support the above.

  • XØ:p provides planning, architecture and related design services to institutional, industrial and commercial clients and partners for the conceptual and physical development of land and buildings.
  • XØ:p provides development, leasing and management services to industrial and commercial clients and partners for buildings that serve the cultural community and creative industries.
  • XØ:p produces and presents performing, visual and literary arts projects and educational programs at The Old American Can Factory complex in Gowanus Brooklyn. There, XØ:p offers an array of uncommon indoor and outdoor spaces for site-specific work, and can provide artistic, technical and logistical support.

HISTORY | HIGHLIGHTS

XØ Projects Inc (XØ:p) continues to grow its integrated knowledge base which span various disciplines related to understanding the built environment. Following are highlights of XØ:p's organizational evolution:

  • XØ:p was founded in 1987 as an architectural planning and design office named, S.P.A.C.E.
  • 1989 | S.P.A.C.E. incorporated as Space Read Inc (SRI) when it partnered with MK Read & Co Inc to develop The Factories at Parlor City in Binghamton, NY and other commercial and industrial projects.
  • 1992 | SRI established The Read Group in another partnership with MK Read and Co Inc to research & develop, manufacture and distribute modular building systems, materials and components for a three-year period. For additional information go to <PRODUCTS> THE READ GROUP.
  • 1996 | SRI continued its planning, architecture and design practice, employing project-based teams and collaborating with other firms. SRI introduces its new name XØ Projects Inc. For additional information go to <PROJECTS > PLANNING + ARCHITECTURE.
  • 2000 | XØ:p began to also produce and present site-specific visual and performing arts projects primarily at The Old American Can Factory, in an effort to explore new forms of "dynamic" architecture. For project information go to <PROJECTS> PERFORMANCE + VISUAL ART.
  • 2003 | XØ:p began offering development services to industrial and commercial property owners as clients and partners in addition to planning and design services. XØ:p began to redevelop The Old American Can Factory in Gowanus as an industrial complex with an emphasis on art and design. For project information go to <PLACES> THE OLD AMERICAN CAN FACTORY.
  • 2007 | XØ:p began to develop INDUSTRIANA®EW as an industrial complex with an emphasis on art and design. For project information go to <PLACES> THE OLD GRAND MORGAN FACTORY.

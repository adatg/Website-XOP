1.0 PROJECT

1.1 SUMMARY

The Old American Can Factory, an historic 130,000 sf, six-building industrial complex at the Gowanus Canal in Brooklyn, is an arts and manufacturing community where more than 300 people are producing a myriad of ideas, experiences and products within the design, arts and culture industry. Curated tenants include a wide range of designers, visual, performing & literary artists and companies as well as manufacturing businesses. For a complete list of people currently working here, please visit <PEOPLE>.

The complex, built between 1865-1901 with a medievalesque courtyard and alleyways, has a quiet, vibrant artistic energy heightened throughout the year by uncommon performances, exhibitions and events presented in and around the buildings. The Old American Can Factory was developed and is operated by XØ Projects Inc.

Workspaces are suitable for light industrial use within the creative industries (except woodworking) or as artist studios and/or offices. Live-work or live only is NOT available at this complex. Rental rates are determined based on studio features, location, use, occupancy and lease term.

1.2 LOCATION

232 Third Street corner Third Avenue in Gowanus, Brooklyn at the edge of the Gowanus Canal's Fifth Street Basin centered between Carroll Gardens and Park Slope, Brooklyn, NY 11215.

Detailed Location and Direction information at <CONTACT>

1.3 BUILDING DESCRIPTION

  • Six interconnecting buildings, 2 to 4 stories with a courtyard at center
  • Masonry and timber construction; Building A with open-web steel roof joists
  • Fully sprinklered, wet system throughout
  • Electric: Heavy 3 phase power available throughout the complex. Not included in base rent
  • Gas: Available throughout the complex. Not included in base rent
  • Three loading docks; heights vary (Bldg C/D/E)
  • Limited/temporary on-site parking; street parking
  • Two freight elevators: one over-sized, automatic with 8,000 lb cap (Bldg C/D/E)
  • Additional on-site storage
  • Scheduled weekly maintenance of common areas
  • Daily rubbish removal
  • Building accessible 24 hrs/365 days
  • Superintendent's office on site
  • Leasing/management office on site

1.4 STUDIO FEATURES (VARIES FROM BUILDING TO BUILDING)

  • Ceiling heights range appx 10’-0" to 20'-0" high
  • Many are entirely column-free
  • Most with large insulated windows
  • Most with steel doors and mortised latch sets & deadbolts
  • 100-400 amp CB panel inside studios, all individually metered or sub-metered
  • Hydronic convection or forced-air heating systems
  • Bathrooms and wash area inside studios or on same floor

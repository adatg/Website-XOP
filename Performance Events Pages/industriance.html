INDUSTRIANCE(TM)

INDUSTRIANCE (TM) is an ongoing series of programs focusing on the changing industrial and agricultural landscape in urban and rural America and beyond.

Performances, films, exhibitions and lectures explore the production and manufacturing sectors of our society and economy, examining the impact of a globalized economy, government policy and property development on the lives and places where things were, could or are still being made.

1.0 SUMMARY

The Old Sackett & Williams Lithographing Factory, to be known as INDUSTRIANA®EW, is a six building, 250,000 SF industrial complex in the Industrial Business Zone (IBZ) in East Williamsburg, Brooklyn. It is currently in its first phase of renovation and redevelopment as a curated production facility for art, design and the creative industries. Live-work or live only will NOT be available at this building. INDUSTRIANA®EW is being developed and will be operated by XØ Projects Inc. Additional information about the history and future of this complex will be added incrementally over the coming months.

2.0 LOCATION

INDUSTRIANA®EW is bound by Metropolitan Ave, Grand St, Morgan Ave and Vandervoort St in Brooklyn, NY 11211. It sits at the eastern edge of Brooklyn, steps from English Kills-Newton Creek near the border with Ridgewood, Queens.

3.0 BUILDINGS

  • Five interconnected buildings, 2-5 stories with cellars. One boiler building with 120' high chimney.
  • Masonry and timber construction
  • Fully sprinklered, wet system throughout
  • Electric: 3 phase power available
  • Gas: Available
  • Loading docks in courtyard
  • Semi-automatic freight elevators
  • Building accessible 24 hrs/365 days
  • Superintendent on site

4.0 FACILITIES

STUDIO FEATURES (VARIES FROM BUILDING TO BUILDING)

  • Ceiling heights range appx 12’-0" to 20'-0" high
  • Many are entirely column-free
  • Most with large insulated windows
  • Most with steel doors and mortised latch sets & deadbolts
  • 80-100 amp CB panel inside studios, all individually metered or sub-metered
  • Hydronic convection or forced-air heating systems
  • Bathrooms and wash area near studios or on same floor
